Introducing Er. Dileesh S

Director & Founder

Shri. Dileesh S began his career as a VHDL programmer at VSSC under Hirel Solutions. Driven by his immense passion for teaching and a vision to mould future generations, he entered the field of education in 2010 by establishing a centre in the heart of Trivandrum city, Kowdiar.

With his exceptional talent in teaching Mathematics and guiding students, the centre quickly expanded to Ambalamukku, Peroorkada, Kazhakuttam, and beyond — now serving thousands of learners. Today, the institution continues its 16-year journey of success with a well-established centre near St. Thomas School, Trivandrum.

His mission is to mould a world of high-class professionals in Engineering and Technology, equipped with immense knowledge and strong values.

Empowering students to achieve academic excellence through personalized learning, expert guidance, and proven teaching methods. Your success is our mission.
